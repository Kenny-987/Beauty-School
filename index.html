About Us

Welcome to Wakanaka Beauty Academy, where we are dedicated to providing top-notch education in the fields of hairdressing, makeup, and nail technology. Our academy is committed to fostering creativity, innovation, and professionalism in our students, ensuring they are prepared to excel in the beauty industry.

Mission Statement

At Wakanaka Beauty Academy, our mission is to empower individuals through high-quality beauty education, equipping them with the skills and confidence to excel in the beauty industry. We are dedicated to fostering creativity, innovation, and professionalism in our students, ensuring they are prepared to meet the demands of a dynamic and evolving market.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading beauty academy recognized for excellence in education, cutting-edge training techniques, and a commitment to the personal and professional growth of our students. We aspire to create a community where aspiring beauty professionals can thrive, innovate, and contribute to the beauty industry with confidence and creativity.

Courses

Hairdressing

Learn the latest hairdressing techniques including cutting, coloring, and styling. This course provides hands-on experience with modern tools and products.

Duration: 2 Months

Cost: R3 200

Wig installation only: R2 500

Starter Kit: Includes scissors, combs, brushes, and basic hair products.

Makeup

Master the art of makeup application from basic to advanced techniques. Topics include skincare, contouring, eye makeup, and special effects makeup.

Duration: 2 Months

Cost: R2 500

Starter Kit: Includes brushes, palettes, foundation, and other essential makeup products.

Nail Technology

Become an expert in nail technology with a focus on manicure, pedicure, acrylic nails, gel nails, and nail art.

Duration: 2 Months

Cost: R3 500

Starter Kit: Includes nail files, clippers, polishes, and UV lamp.

Massage

Learn various massage techniques including Swedish, deep tissue, and aromatherapy. This course emphasizes relaxation and therapeutic practices.

Duration: 1 Month

Cost: R3 000

Starter Kit: Includes oils, lotions, and massage tools

Eyelash Extensions

Learn the art of wig installation and styling, including lace front wigs and weaves.

Duration: 1 Month

Cost: R3 200

Starter Kit: Includes wig caps, styling tools, and adhesives
